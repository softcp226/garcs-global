About Us

International Fund Recovery.

We assist individuals and organizations in resolving international fund holds, tax compliance issues, and regulatory release procedures.

Global Asset Recovery & Compliance Services (GARCS) is a private compliance and regulatory support firm specializing in international fund clearance, cross-border tax resolution, and regulatory documentation processing.

We assist clients in securing the release of funds temporarily held due to regulatory, tax, or cross-border verification procedures. Tax Exemption & Origin Certification We prepare and submit documentation confirming non-U.S. income sources and eligibility for tax exemption under international financial regulations.
